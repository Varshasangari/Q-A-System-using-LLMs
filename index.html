About This Project

🧠 What are Large Language Models?

Large Language Models (LLMs) are advanced deep learning models trained on massive amounts of text data. They use transformer architectures with billions of parameters to understand and generate human-like text. These models learn patterns, relationships, and contextual understanding from their training data.

🚀 Why Question-Answering Systems Matter

QA systems represent one of the most practical applications of AI, bridging the gap between human curiosity and machine intelligence. They're used in customer support, education, research, healthcare, and countless other domains where quick, accurate information access is crucial.

📖 Educational Value

This project demonstrates core concepts in natural language processing, deep learning, and full-stack web development. It showcases how modern AI can be integrated into accessible web applications, making advanced technology available to everyone through an intuitive interface.
